# 📊 Customer Analytics Report (Excel Dashboard)

## 📝 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive *customer analytics dashboard* built using *Microsoft Excel. The dashboard provides insights into customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and wealth segmentation, with a focus on **bike-related purchases*.

The goal of this analysis is to uncover patterns that can support *data-driven decision-making*, improve customer targeting, and optimize business strategies.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel  
  - Pivot Tables  
  - Charts & Visualizations  
  - Data Cleaning & Transformation  
  - Dashboard Design  

---

## 📌 Key Metrics
- *Average Bike Purchases (3 Years):* 48.89  
- *Average Customer Tenure:* 10.66 years  
- *Car Ownership Rate:* 50.60%  
- *Deceased Indicator Count:* 2  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Insights

### 👥 Gender Distribution
- Female customers slightly dominate at *50.98%*
- Male customers account for *46.83%*
- Unknown category is minimal (*2.20%*)

*Insight:* Customer base is fairly balanced, with a slight female majority.

---

### 🎂 Age Distribution
- Highest concentration in *43–52 age group (1410 customers)*
- Followed by:
  - 53–62 (700)
  - 33–42 (682)

*Insight:* Middle-aged customers are the primary contributors, indicating strong purchasing power in this segment.

---

### 🚴 Average Bike Purchases
- Peak purchase range: *1–4 (49.77)*  
- Slight decline as range increases  
- Lowest at *21–24 (46.12)*  

*Insight:* Lower purchase frequency groups show higher averages, suggesting frequent buyers may reduce spending over time.

---

### 💼 Top Job Titles
- Business Systems Analyst (45)
- Tax Accountant (44)
- Social Worker (44)
- Internal Auditor (42)

*Insight:* Customers are largely professionals in structured and analytical roles.

---

### 🏭 Job Industry Distribution
Top industries:
- Manufacturing (799)
- Financial Services (774)
- Health (602)

*Insight:* Majority of customers come from industrial and finance sectors, indicating stable income groups.

---

### 💰 Wealth Segmentation
- Mass Customers: *50.00%*
- High Net Worth: *25.53%*
- Affluent Customers: *24.48%*

*Insight:* Business is largely driven by mass-market customers.

---

### 🚴 Bike Purchases by Wealth Segment
- Mass Customers: *49.73 (highest)*
- Affluent Customers: *48.85*
- High Net Worth: *47.29 (lowest)*

*Insight:* Mass-market customers are the most active buyers.

---

### ⏳ Average Tenure by Wealth Segment
- High Net Worth: *10.75 years*
- Mass Customers: *10.69 years*
- Affluent Customers: *10.52 years*

*Insight:* High-value customers tend to stay slightly longer.

---

### ⚠️ Deceased Impact on Purchases
- No: *195,387*
- Yes: *173*

*Insight:* Minimal impact on overall purchasing trends.

---

## 📈 Key Takeaways
- Middle-aged customers (43–52) are the most valuable segment  
- Mass-market customers drive the highest purchase volume  
- Manufacturing and financial sectors dominate customer base  
- Customer tenure is relatively stable across all segments  
- Purchase behavior slightly declines with increased frequency  

---

## 🚀 Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on *middle-aged professionals*  
- Develop targeted campaigns for *mass-market customers*  
- Introduce loyalty programs to maintain long-term customers  
- Explore strategies to increase engagement among high net worth individuals  
- Leverage industry-based segmentation for personalized marketing
